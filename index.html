Health Impact Guide

Understand AQI levels and precautions

Good (0-50)

Air quality is satisfactory, little to no risk.

Moderate (51-100)

Acceptable; sensitive individuals may experience minor symptoms.

Unhealthy for Sensitive (101-150)

Members of sensitive groups may experience health effects.

Unhealthy (151-200)

Everyone may begin to experience health effects.

Very Unhealthy (201-300)

Health alert: everyone may experience more serious effects.

Clean Air Resources

Tips & guides to reduce exposure

Indoor Air Quality

Use HEPA filters, maintain ventilation, avoid indoor pollutants.

Protective Gear

N95 masks reduce particle exposure during high pollution days.

Green Spaces

Visit parks away from traffic; indoor plants improve air.

Low Emission Travel

Use public transport, carpool, or electric vehicles.
